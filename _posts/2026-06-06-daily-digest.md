## arXiv CS.AI

**[PSEBench: A Controllable and Verifiable Benchmark for Evaluating LLMs in Patient Safety Event Triage](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.05463)**  
# PSEBench: A New Gold Standard for Testing AI in Patient Safety

Researchers have created PSEBench, a groundbreaking benchmark that finally gives us a rigorous way to evaluate whether AI systems can reliably handle the critical task of patient safety event triage—determining which clinical incidents must be reported under complex regulatory rules. Built on "clause cards" that translate confusing policy language into auditable decision logic, this framework ensures LLMs don't just guess right, but actually reason through evidence and know when to admit uncertainty in genuinely ambiguous cases. This matters: it's a major step toward deploying AI in healthcare contexts where getting it wrong literally costs lives.
