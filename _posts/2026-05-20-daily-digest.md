## arXiv CS.AI

**[Agentic Trading: When LLM Agents Meet Financial Markets](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.19337)**  
# Summary

Researchers are mapping how LLM agents are transforming financial trading by acting as intelligent decision-makers that perceive markets, reason through options, and adapt in real time—but a new audit reveals a critical gap: most studies use incompatible methodologies, making it nearly impossible to compare which approaches actually work. This systematic evidence map of 77 studies identifies the handful of rigorous, reproducible frameworks that could standardize how we build and evaluate AI trading agents, setting the stage for more reliable AI in one of the highest-stakes industries. The takeaway? The breakthrough isn't just that LLMs can trade—it's that we're finally getting serious about proving *which* ones can trade reliably.
