## arXiv CS.AI

**[AgentAtlas: Beyond Outcome Leaderboards for LLM Agents](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.20530)**  
# AgentAtlas: Beyond Outcome Leaderboards for LLM Agents

AgentAtlas moves AI agent evaluation beyond simple win-loss metrics, introducing a comprehensive taxonomy that captures *how* agents fail and *why*—covering everything from decision-making patterns to safety and robustness across real-world environments like codebases, browsers, and operating systems. This shift from single-number benchmarks to nuanced, multi-dimensional assessment is a game-changer for deploying agents that need to be trustworthy, not just lucky. Finally, we have a framework that lets builders understand agent behavior deeply enough to ship with confidence.
